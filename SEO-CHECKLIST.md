## ✅ STEP 2 — Submit your sitemap (2 min)

**What it is:** A sitemap is a list of all your pages. Submitting it = handing Google
a map so it finds every page.

1. In Search Console, left menu → **Sitemaps**.
2. In the box, type just this and click **Submit**:
   ```
   sitemap.xml
   ```
3. Done. It may say "Couldn't fetch" for a few hours — that's fine, check back later;
   it usually flips to "Success" on its own.

## 📊 How to check if it's working (free, anytime)

After ~2–3 weeks, in Search Console click the **"Performance"** tab. It shows:

- **Total clicks** — how many people clicked through to your site
- **Total impressions** — how many times you appeared in Google
- **Queries** — the exact words people typed to find you, and your average **position**
  (1 = top of page 1; lower number = better)

👉 **The simple rule:** look for words where you're at **position 5–15** with lots of
impressions. You're *almost* on page 1 for those — small wins. Note them down.
